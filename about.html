About


I undertook the task of creating the "Almost 3" Autism Transition Tool as a response to my own family's experience transitioning out of Early Intervention. I had a hard time adjusting to life after EI because I had no way of envisioning what our future -- or, our "now" -- would look like.

This resource was originally based on, "Starting Points for Service Coordinators for Children with Hearing Loss." The pursuit of this project was inspired by my EI Social Worker, a pretty amazing woman that helped me survive that first year after my child's diagnosis. She suggested I create a tool like this one and I'm so glad that she did.

This project is the outcome of my participation in the Early Intervention Parent Leadership Project's Family Leadership Training Series funded by the Massachusetts Department of Public Health. I created this tool with the guidance, help, and support of the training's facilitators and my fellow participants. I also received significant input from other caregivers and professionals in the field.

I look forward to further refining this tool with additional input and support from more caregivers and professionals in the field. I welcome your feedback and would love to hear from you. You can reach me at sophia@AutismTransitionTools.org.
