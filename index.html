Welcome to the Autism Transition Tool

As our children grow and age, there will be many transitions. Ending Early Intervention (EI) services is the first of many of these types of changes that we, as parents and guardians, must weather.

The purpose of this tool is to empower you in building a greater vision of the services, supports, and community-based programs that will help sustain your family as you all grow and age.

The following questions are designed to help you assess what services you are receiving in EI and what services you are looking forward to once your child ages out of EI. Once you have completed all the questions, you will be directed to a final page that includes your answers and resources that may be helpful.
