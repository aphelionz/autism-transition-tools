"Almost 3" Autism Transition Tool

This is a survey-style questionnaire designed to assist caregivers (including parents, grandparents, aunts or uncles, foster parents, etc.) of children diagnosed with an Autism Spectrum Disorder who live in Massachusetts with their transition out of Early Intervention (EI).

It takes approximately 15-20 minutes to complete 6 sections of questions. At the end of the 6th section, you will be directed to a "Results" page, which will include some suggestions about next steps you can take and information about some helpful resources.

Don't have time?

You can access the "Caregivers' Guide to Transitioning Out of EI for Children with Autism." Either print or email yourself a copy.

Welcome!



The purpose of the "Almost 3" Autism Transition Tool is to accomplish four things;

1. To compare current services and supports with those that will be in place after EI.

2. To identify areas where support might be lacking in the future.

3. To provide information about some helpful resources.

4. To provide suggestions about how to utilize current providers in creating a strong, supportive network of services and community connections for the family.



Please consider providing feedback by clicking "Submit Feedback" when you are done.
