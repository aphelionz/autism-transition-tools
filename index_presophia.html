Introduction

As our children grow and age, there will be many transitions. Ending Early Intervention services is the first of many of these types of changes that we, as caregivers, must weather. This tool is designed to empower you as you enter, proceed through, and complete this process.

The purpose of this tool is to empower you to build a greater vision of the services, supports, and community-based programs that will help sustain your family as you all grow and age.

This tool is specifically designed for caregivers of children on the autism spectrum, although parents of children with other diagnoses might find it helpful, too.

At the end of this survey, this survey and resources can be printed out. - unless there’s a good descriptor, it’ll be complicated for users to understand what im trying to do; define target audience.
